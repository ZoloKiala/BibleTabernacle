Welcome to Bible Tabernacle

Bible Tabernacle PMB & DBN is a non-denominational Bible-believing church. We fellowship around the Word of God as revealed in this end-time season. We have “no creed but Christ, no law but love, and no book but the Bible”. We fellowship around the Word of God as revealed in this end-time season by the Holy Spirit.

We have been awakened by the great shepherd’s voice for our day made known through the ministry of Malachi 4:5-6; Revelations 10:7 and Luke 17:30. As Abraham sent Eliezer to get a bride, Rebecca for his only son Isaac, God sent a Prophet/Messenger, Reverend William Marrion Branham (1909-1965) to prepare a Bride for His only begotten Son, even our Lord Jesus Christ.

Come fellowship with us if you would like to be a part of the body of believers living and spreading the Word of our soon returning Lord and King Jesus Christ
